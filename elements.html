My work on the Smart Agriculture System demonstrates how IoT technology can revolutionize traditional farming practices. By integrating soil moisture sensors, temperature and humidity monitors, and automated irrigation controls, I've created a comprehensive solution that helps farmers optimize resource usage while improving crop yields. The system collects environmental data in real-time, processes it through advanced algorithms, and provides actionable insights through an intuitive mobile application. This approach not only reduces water consumption but also minimizes the need for chemical interventions, promoting more sustainable agricultural practices.

Right Aligned

Another significant project in my portfolio is the Potato Leaf Disease Classification system, which uses machine learning and computer vision techniques to identify plant diseases at early stages. By analyzing images of potato leaves, the algorithm can detect various diseases including early blight and late blight with over 92% accuracy. This early detection capability allows farmers to implement targeted treatments before diseases spread throughout their crops, significantly reducing losses and chemical usage. The system is accessible through a mobile application, making advanced technology available to farmers regardless of their technical expertise.
